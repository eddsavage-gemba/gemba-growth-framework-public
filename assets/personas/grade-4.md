# Focus

They lead teams or workstreams, shaping ways of working, maintaining psychological safety, and ensuring outcomes align with client goals. They balance delivery, people development, and standards.

# Attributes
- Lead teams with clarity, empathy, and authority
- Delegate effectively and create growth opportunities for others
- Manage feedback cycles and performance constructively
- Act as a primary client delivery lead
- Own both the work and how the work gets done
- Lead recruitment activities and contribute to hiring strategy

# What it's like to work with them
They create clarity. You know what good looks like, why decisions were made, and where you have room to act.

They balance delivery with people care, give feedback early and fairly, and protect the team from unnecessary noise. Under pressure, they stay grounded and help others do the same.

# What it feels like working with them
- You can raise issues without fear
- Priorities are clear
- You're stretched, not stressed

# This is someone...

You trust to run a team and deliver reliably under pressure.
