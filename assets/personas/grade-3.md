# Focus

They operate fully autonomously, solve complex or ambiguous problems, and are sought out for their judgement. They mentor others, contribute to design and decision-making, and materially improve quality, risk management, or outcomes.

# Attributes
- Handle complex, ambiguous problems independently
- Provide technical or professional leadership without line management
- Mentor and coach others; help peers make better decisions
- Understand the client mission deeply and influence priorities
- Ensure quality standards are met or exceeded
- Actively contribute reusable assets, patterns, or approaches

# What it's like to work with them
They're the person you go to when things are unclear or tricky. They're calm in ambiguity, break down complex problems, and make sensible decisions without drama.

They're generous with their knowledge and help others think better rather than just giving answers. When they're involved, quality goes up and risks go down.

# What it feels like working with them
- They reduce chaos and uncertainty
- They make the whole team better
- You rely on their judgement

# This is someone...

You trust to make hard problems smaller for everyone else.
