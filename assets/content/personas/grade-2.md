# Focus

They can own moderately complex work end-to-end and make informed decisions within their domain. They understand client needs well enough to make sensible trade-offs and suggest improvements to quality or approach.

# Attributes
- Work independently day-to-day with minimal oversight
- Apply best practices confidently and explain their reasoning
- Communicate clearly with peers and sometimes clients
- Take responsibility for their own development and learning
- Contribute actively to team culture and knowledge sharing
- Begin contributing to recruitment (interviews, assessments)

# What it's like to work with them
They're steady and dependable. You can give them a piece of work and trust it will move forward without constant check-ins.

They think things through, explain their reasoning, and flag trade-offs rather than surprising you late. They manage their own client interactions sensibly and take ownership of their development.

# What it feels like working with them
- They don't need micromanaging
- They explain why, not just what
- You trust them to handle complexity at their level

# This is someone...

You trust to get things done well without hand-holding.
