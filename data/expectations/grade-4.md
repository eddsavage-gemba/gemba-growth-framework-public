# Growth and Craft

- Provides structured development support across the whole team.
- Delegates effectively to enable growth and create opportunities for others.
- Provides balanced, developmental feedback to help people move forward.
- Actively monitors team health and workload, intervening early to prevent burnout or unsustainable practices.

# Personal Effectiveness

- Leads team culture and ways of working; fosters psychological safety.
- Communicates with clarity and authority, internally and externally.
- Manages constructive feedback cycles within the team (giving, receiving, facilitating).
- Addresses team issues early and keeps the team aligned and focused.
- Maintains a high bar for themselves in standards, performance, and growth.
- Recruitment: Leads recruitment activities and contributes to hiring strategy.

# Client Value and Impact

- Acts as the main delivery lead for the client and represents Gemba well.
- Makes sure the team understands the client's goals and delivers against them.
- Sets clear expectations and guides client decision-making.
- Spots risks and opportunities that really matter to delivery or mission success.

# Delivery Quality

- Gives clear technical or professional direction to teams.
- Sense-checks solutions and ensures good practice.
- Balances quality, speed, and risk thoughtfully.
- Guides teams through complex decisions and uncertainty.
- Owns both the work and the experience of the people doing it.
- Helps teams make decisions by setting clear intent and boundaries.
- Makes trade-offs visible to clients and teams.

# Community and Contribution

- Drives communities of practice or significant internal initiatives.
- Creates reusable patterns, frameworks, or knowledge resources.
- Represents Gemba externally with thought leadership or community leadership.
