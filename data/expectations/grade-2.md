# Growth and Craft

- Takes ownership of personal development and identifies skill gaps proactively.
- Helps onboard or support juniors through pairing, explaining tasks, or sharing experience.
- Adopts good habits that contribute to team learning (docs, walkthroughs, demos).
- Takes on harder or less-defined tasks to expand capability.
- Regularly shares new learning, contributing to team growth.
- Proactively deepens their craft (tools, practices, domain details).
- Maintains a sustainable pace and manages personal energy to deliver consistently over time.

# Personal Effectiveness

- Works autonomously day-to-day while contributing strongly to team collaboration.
- Communicates clearly with peers and occasionally with clients.
- Gives constructive feedback to peers in a respectful, supportive way.
- Demonstrates judgement on when to seek clarifications or escalate issues.
- Recruitment: Contributes (interviews, feedback, assessment tasks).

# Client Value and Impact

- Understands the client's mission well enough to make good day-to-day decisions.
- Manages their own communication with the client confidently.
- Suggests improvements that help quality or outcomes.
